🏋️‍♂️ Active Directory

Mastering Active Directory is mastering the keys to the corporate kingdom.

🧠 About the Course

This course introduces offensive concepts related to Active Directory (AD), Microsoft's centralized identity and access management system. AD is a prime target during internal penetration tests and Red Team engagements due to its critical role in enterprise environments.

You’ll explore domain enumeration, privilege escalation, credential abuse, and post-exploitation techniques — all essential for offensive operators in Windows enterprise infrastructures.

🎯 Objectives

  • Understand the structure and components of Active Directory
  • Identify and exploit common misconfigurations
  • Enumerate AD using built-in tools and custom scripts
  • Escalate privileges and move laterally through the domain
  • Abuse certificate services and maintain persistence

🧩 Course Topics

🔍 Enumeration Techniques

Gather domain info, users, SPNs, trust relationships, GPOs, and permissions.

💥 Exploitation & Lateral Movement

Use techniques like pass-the-hash, Kerberoasting, overpass-the-hash, and more.

🛠️ Post-Exploitation

Dump credentials, maintain persistence, extract secrets and elevate access further.

🏅 AD CS & Certificate Abuse

Abuse misconfigured certificate templates and privilege escalation paths in AD CS.

🧱 Offensive AD Skill Blocks

🚪 Initial Access

Get into the network via phishing, rogue devices, leaked credentials, or VPN entry points.

🔎 Enumeration

Use LDAP, Kerberos, SMB, RPC, and more to map out the domain and locate privilege paths.

🔐 Credential Abuse

Dump LSASS, reuse NTLM hashes, crack SPNs, perform pass-the-hash or pass-the-ticket attacks.

🏃‍♂️ Lateral Movement

Move across systems using PsExec, WinRM, RDP, WMI, or DCOM techniques.

🏆 Domain Privilege Escalation

Abuse ACLs, GPOs, constrained delegation, or misconfigurations to reach Domain Admin.

🎖️ Certificate Attacks

Use ESC1–ESC8 techniques to escalate privileges or persist via AD Certificate Services.

Active Directory is both a fortress and a maze — mastering it empowers you to breach with precision.

What is cybersecurity research?

In cybersecurity, research is not just academic — it's a daily skill. It includes everything from staying updated on vulnerabilities, to investigating new technologies, to crafting tailored exploits in real-world scenarios. This section explores different angles of research used by offensive and defensive professionals alike.

As a pentester, SOC analyst, or Red Team operator, being able to quickly gather, validate, and apply technical intelligence is critical to success.

Explore the Research Methodologies

🛰️ Technology Watch

Organize your continuous learning: CVEs, advisories, security blogs, exploit databases, YouTube channels, and more.

🔎 Vulnerability Research

Identify software or service versions and determine if they are affected by public exploits or CVEs.

💣 Exploit Development

Write your own exploit for a unique or custom vulnerability (0-day like). Craft payloads with precision.

Technology Watch & Continuous Learning

"Research is the silent force behind every successful attack and every prevented breach."

Staying up-to-date is not an option in cybersecurity — it's a daily commitment. Whether you're a SOC analyst, a pentester, or a blue teamer, tracking vulnerabilities, tools, techniques, and real incidents will sharpen your skills and make you more relevant.

1. Official Sources & Cyber Threat Intelligence

  • ANSSI (French CERT) — advisories, security alerts, publications
  • CISA Advisories — U.S. government threat intelligence
  • Europol Cybercrime Reports
  • AlienVault OTX — threat sharing platform
  • vx-underground — malware & threat intel community updates

2. Exploit & Vulnerability Databases

  • Exploit-DB — real public exploits
  • NVD — U.S. National Vulnerability Database
  • CVE Details — searchable CVE database
  • PoC-in-GitHub — GitHub PoCs for new CVEs

3. Bug Bounty & Public Reports

  • HackerOne Hacktivity — real reports and vulnerabilities
  • Bugcrowd Disclosures
  • YesWeHack — European Bug Bounty platform
  • Intigriti — selected bug bounty programs

4. Forums & Knowledge Sharing

  • Stack Overflow — code-level troubleshooting & learning
  • Security StackExchange — community Q&A
  • Hack The Box Forum
  • Reddit (r/netsec, r/bugbounty, r/cybersecurity)

5. Learning Platforms

  • TryHackMe — hands-on labs and paths
  • HTB Academy
  • OpenClassrooms — general IT & security fundamentals
  • OverTheWire — Linux & CTF basics

6. YouTube Channels & Podcasts

  • NahamSec — Bug bounty, recon techniques, and live hacking events
  • NetworkChuck — Networking, cloud, coffee-powered labs and fun experiments
  • rs0n_live — Web Bug Bounty deep dives
  • InsiderPhD — Bug bounty walkthroughs, recon tips, and mindset insights
  • Underscore — IT world activity
  • HackSmarter — Pentest methodology, education

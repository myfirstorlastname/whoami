üß≠ Mini Playbooks

1) Red Team Recon (OSINT ‚Üí Validation)

  • People & Org: public org chart, job posts (stack + tools), LinkedIn roles for phishing personas.
  • Infra: target domains, subdomains, ASN/cloud providers, known tech (CDN/WAF, SSO, mail gateways).
  • Exposures: dev portals, forgotten staging hosts, VPN portals, OWA/SSO, default logins.
  • Validate: confirm login portals, observe headers/cookies, check rate limits & lockout hints.

2) Weaponization (Delivery ‚Üí Execution)

  • File types: containerized formats (ISO/VHD) vs. doc macros, shortcut chains (LNK ‚Üí LOLBins), JS/HTA.
  • Staging: redirectors, signed-like URLs, content-type control; prefer pull models over big attachments.
  • OPSEC: compress/obfuscate only as needed, randomize filenames & compile times, avoid common IoCs.

3) Password Attacks (Beginner Focus)

  • Credential stuffing: reuse leaked combos on target SSO/VPN/OWA; filter by target domain first.
  • Password spraying: 1‚Äì3 common candidates across many users; respect lockout thresholds; add jitter and sleep.
  • Wordlists & rules: build with company thematics (brand, season, year), apply rules (Spring2025! variants) sparingly.
  • MFA reality: look for legacy protocols, backup codes, or MFA fatigue vectors‚Äîbut keep noise minimal.
  • Validation: detect soft signals (status/length changes), don‚Äôt chase false positives without a second check.

4) Phishing (Pretext ‚Üí Delivery ‚Üí Capture)

  • Pretext: pick business-credible triggers (policy updates, invoice queries, calendar conflicts).
  • Trust: domains that pass SPF/DKIM/DMARC; clone tone & cadence from real comms.
  • Delivery: link-first (credential capture) vs. attachment-first (execution) depending on objective & OPSEC.
  • Capture: unique tokens per user, short-lived links, clear telemetry; handle credentials responsibly.

üß© Defense Mindset (for blue & purple)

  • Instrument login portals: per-user and per-IP thresholds, geo/time anomalies, progressive friction instead of hard locks only.
  • Harden email: enforce DMARC quarantine/reject, banner external senders, train on tone mismatch cues.
  • Constrain execution: block scriptable containers, AMSI + Attack Surface Reduction rules, macro policies with MOTW enforcement.
  • Measure detection debt: what gets through today? test with controlled exercises.

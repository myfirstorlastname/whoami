  • X-Frame-Options: Set to DENY or SAMEORIGIN to prevent framing.
  • Content-Security-Policy (CSP) frame-ancestors: Modern, fine-grained control over allowed parent frames.
  • Frame-busting scripts: JS to detect/escape framing (helpful, but historically bypassable).
  • Re-auth / Confirm UI: Require explicit re-authentication or secondary confirmation for critical actions.
  • UI randomization: Reduce predictable alignment for click targets (defense-in-depth).

    • I built strong foundations in Linux, HTTP protocols, file permissions, and command-line exploitation.
    • I consistently avoided hints to push myself further and better simulate real-world problem solving.

  • This phase sharpened my understanding of real-world attack vectors and strengthened my debugging and automation skills.

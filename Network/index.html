📖 What is a Network?

Professional definition (Cisco):
“Computer networking refers to connected computing devices (such as laptops, desktops, servers, smartphones, and tablets) and an ever-expanding array of IoT devices (such as cameras, door locks, doorbells, refrigerators, audio/visual systems, thermostats, and various sensors) that communicate with one another.”

In plain English:

  • A network is a group of computers and devices that talk to each other.
  • It’s what lets your phone access the internet or send emails.
  • It connects your laptop to your printer, or your office to a cloud service.
  • Big companies use large networks to control how data flows securely between departments and systems.

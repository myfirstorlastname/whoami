Objective: Become an All-Rounder Pentester in 1 Year

Beyond mastering Web, AD, and Linux systems, becoming a well-rounded pentester means gaining competence across multiple domains.

  • Mobile Security : Android & iOS app analysis, traffic interception, reverse engineering
  • Network Pentesting : VLAN hopping, router abuse, MITM attacks, rogue devices
  • Container Security : Docker, Kubernetes, escaping containers, cloud-native attacks
  • Cloud Pentesting : AWS, Azure, identity abuse, misconfigurations, privilege escalation
  • Client-side Attacks : Java/Node/React vulnerabilities, Electron apps, deserialization
  • Reporting & Communication : Clear, actionable, and professional pentest reports

After the OSCP, I plan to work through targeted labs and rooms to fill each of these gaps—one focused topic at a time.

Offense requires creativity, curiosity, and consistency. The goal isn’t just root access—it’s full understanding of how systems fall apart.
